Products

  • Ice Cream

    A rich, sweet, creamy frozen food made from variously flavored cream and milk products...

    Ingredients for the vanilla ice cream

    • 2 cups whipping cream
    • 2 cups half-and-half cream
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    • Freeze according to manufacturer's directions

  • Ice Coffee

    Iced Coffee: the ultimate and tasty refreshment. Is one of the most popular drinks for hot weather.

    Ingredients for the delicious ice coffe

    • Pour the melted ice cream into an empty ice cube tray and freeze until frozen solid, about
      2 hours.
    • Transfer the ice cream cubes to 2 tall glasses and pour 1/2 cup coffee into each.

  • Milk Shake

    A milk shake is a cold drink made by mixing milk with a flavoring or fruit, and sometimes ice cream.

    Ingredients for the vannila milk shake

    • 4 large scoops (about 1 1/2 c.) vanilla ice cream
    • 1/4 c. milk
    • In a blender, blend together
      ice cream and milk
    • Pour into a glass and serve

tradition and love

how it's made?

We have been working for 10 years. Our clients become our partners and bring their loved ones. We are very proud of our achievements.

How did we get started? First we opened our first cafe, it was very scary that people would not like our products. We have established contacts with certified suppliers of homemade dairy products. We developed, opened more and more cafes and we decided to open our own farm.

You can see them on the map: clicking here will take you to the map. We have developed a network of deliveries to the home, to the office.

You motivate us by buying our products, we are very happy to see your happy eyes with a milkshake in your hands. If you want to learn more about our products, share ideas or new recipes, send contacts.
